About

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer and Information Sciences at the Department of Computing Security, Rochester Institute of Technology, under the guidance of Prof. Ivan de Oliveira. My educational background includes a Bachelor of Engineering degree from the State University of Campinas, where I majored in Control and Automation Engineering.

Prior to beginning my Ph.D. program, I worked as a researcher on the Artificial Intelligence team at the Samsung R&D Institute in Brazil. During my time at Samsung, I conducted research primarily focused on Computer Vision and AI applications for Healthcare.

My current research focuses on exploring different aspects of binary and runtime integrity to improve the security of computer systems. Specifically, I am investigating a range of topics related to Remote Attestation, Control Flow Attestation, Authentication, blending attestation with communication protocols, and Embedded and Mobile Security.
